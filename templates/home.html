Home Page

We often find ourselves in a situation where we can not remember the title of the movie that we have watched in the past. We often want to look at it again because we enjoyed watching it, but we can't find it anywhere on the web. That we are going to remember every movie we've ever seen, we're introducing to you online application Movie App. This app gives you a chance to easily add movie information and displays them transparently on your personal list. Now you can easily find the title of the movie, see the relase year, read the comments you wrote, the rating you provided and simply trough the link see the movie trailer or the IMDb page.



About:

If you would like to find out more about how to use this web application and who created it use navigation on the top of the page or below to go on 'About' page.

Add Movie:

If you want to add a movie to your list navigate yourself on a 'Add Movie' site. There you can provide information about a movie that you will be able to access on 'My Movie List' site.

My Movie List:

And last if you want to see your personal movie list go on 'My Movie List' site. There you can see all the movies you've added to the list. By clicking on a movie from the list you can access to all information you have given on the entry. On this page you can also edit and delete movies from your list.
