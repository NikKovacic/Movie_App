About Movie App


Our app gives you opportunity to saves films you have already watched on your personal movie list. You can now rate your favorites movies, saves the date, you have watched them or added them to your list and see the comments you write. With movie app you will never forget the movie you have already seen.



How to use it?

STEP 1:

STEP 2:

Add a movie to a list.

STEP 3:

Find it later.
